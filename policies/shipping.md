# Shipping Policy

## Shipping Options

### Standard Shipping
- **Cost**: Free on orders over $50, otherwise $5.99
- **Delivery Time**: 5-7 business days
- **Tracking**: Included

### Express Shipping
- **Cost**: $12.99
- **Delivery Time**: 2-3 business days
- **Tracking**: Included with real-time updates

### Overnight Shipping
- **Cost**: $24.99
- **Delivery Time**: Next business day (orders placed before 2 PM)
- **Tracking**: Included with real-time updates

## Processing Time
- Orders are processed within 1-2 business days
- Orders placed after 2 PM or on weekends are processed the next business day

## Shipping Carriers
We partner with major carriers including:
- UPS
- FedEx
- USPS

## International Shipping
- Currently available to Canada and Mexico only
- Delivery time: 7-14 business days
- Customer responsible for customs duties and taxes

## Tracking Your Order
1. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships
2. Track your package on our website or the carrier's website
3. Sign up for SMS updates for real-time notifications

## Delivery Issues

### Package Not Received
If your tracking shows delivered but you haven't received your package:
1. Check with neighbors and building management
2. Look for a delivery notice
3. Contact us within 7 days of the delivery date

### Address Changes
Address changes can only be made within 1 hour of placing your order. After that, please contact customer support.

## PO Boxes
We ship to PO Boxes via USPS only. Express and overnight shipping are not available for PO Box addresses.
