# Return Policy

## Overview
We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we're here to help.

## Return Window
- **Standard items**: 30 days from delivery date
- **Electronics**: 15 days from delivery date
- **Sale items**: 14 days from delivery date (final sale items excluded)

## Conditions for Return
Items must be:
- In original, unopened packaging
- Unused and in resalable condition
- Accompanied by original receipt or order confirmation

## How to Initiate a Return
1. Log into your account and go to "Order History"
2. Select the order and click "Return Items"
3. Choose the items and reason for return
4. Print the prepaid shipping label
5. Drop off at any authorized shipping location

## Refund Processing
- **Original payment method**: 5-7 business days after we receive the item
- **Store credit**: Issued within 24 hours of return approval

## Non-Returnable Items
- Gift cards
- Downloadable software
- Items marked as "Final Sale"
- Personal care items that have been opened
- Custom or personalized items

## Damaged or Defective Items
If you received a damaged or defective item, contact us within 48 hours of delivery for a full refund or replacement at no extra cost.
