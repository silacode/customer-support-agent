# Warranty Policy

## Standard Warranty Coverage

All products sold through our store come with a manufacturer's warranty:

| Category | Warranty Period |
|----------|-----------------|
| Electronics | 1 year |
| Accessories | 6 months |
| Refurbished items | 90 days |

## What's Covered
- Manufacturing defects
- Hardware malfunctions under normal use
- Component failures not caused by user damage

## What's NOT Covered
- Physical damage (drops, spills, cracks)
- Damage from misuse or unauthorized modifications
- Normal wear and tear
- Cosmetic damage that doesn't affect functionality
- Damage from power surges (unless using approved surge protector)

## Extended Warranty
Purchase extended protection for added peace of mind:
- **1 Additional Year**: $19.99
- **2 Additional Years**: $34.99

Extended warranty must be purchased within 30 days of original order.

## How to Make a Warranty Claim

1. **Gather Information**
   - Order number
   - Product serial number (if applicable)
   - Photos or videos showing the issue

2. **Contact Support**
   - Email: warranty@support.com
   - Phone: 1-800-555-0199
   - Live chat on our website

3. **Assessment**
   - We may request you ship the item for inspection
   - Prepaid shipping label provided for valid claims

4. **Resolution**
   - Repair: 7-14 business days
   - Replacement: Ships within 3 business days
   - Refund: Processed within 5 business days

## Proof of Purchase
Keep your order confirmation email as proof of purchase. We can also look up orders using your email address.

## Transferability
Warranty is tied to the original purchaser and is non-transferable.
